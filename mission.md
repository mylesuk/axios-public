# Axios — mission

*A single paragraph: what Axios is, who it is for, and what discipline holds it. Not a strategy memo, a positioning brief, or a list of deliverables — those live in the other docs in `Atelier/00-System/`. Read this when a friend asks what you are building, when you are uncertain whether a new feature or piece of work serves the project, or before walking the findings of an `/audit`.*

---

Axios is a thinking studio I'm building for the long horizon. The aim is to think slowly and honestly on the questions that have always mattered — what makes a good life, what wealth is for, what counts as real knowledge, what classical literature still teaches the modern reader — and to publish only the work that survives my own scrutiny. Each insight names the condition under which it would break, so the work I share is honest about its limits as well as its strength. The articles, threads, and short pieces are the overflow. The vault and the discipline of careful thinking are the actual work.

---

## Where this paragraph is used

- **External.** The answer when a friend (or stranger) asks *"what are you building?"* Lift it whole, or trim to the first two sentences for short contexts.
- **Internal.** The north star for `/audit` and for any moment of doubt about whether something belongs in the vault. If a proposed change does not serve this paragraph, it probably does not serve the project.
- **For Grok.** `sync-axios-public.sh` copies this file to `axios-public/mission.md`. The public repo’s **`mission-primer.md`** is a model-facing *application* guide for this paragraph — it must not replace it. Grok’s read order and paste text live in the vault beside the name **`axios-public-sync`** (checklist note, same folder as the Grok instruction files). When mission *meaning* shifts, update **`mission-primer.md`** and **`glossary.md`** in `axios-public` in the same pass.

## When to revisit

After the first `/audit`, after major structural changes, or when a year has passed. A mission paragraph that has not been re-read in a year is no longer a mission — it is wallpaper. Re-read aloud; if any sentence rings hollow or any phrasing has gone stale, edit in place and append the change to [`decision-log.md`](decision-log.md).

## Discipline

- **One paragraph.** If the paragraph wants to grow, the energy belongs in the pillar docs (`philosophical-content-system.md`, `value-philosophy.md`, `persona.md`, `voice.md`, `positioning.md`), not here. The compression is the point.
- **Written for a friend, not for an audience.** No marketing register, no positioning hooks, no claim about what the system will become. Plain prose that holds up at dinner-table pace.
- **Honest, not aspirational.** If a sentence describes a future state rather than the actual current discipline, it is a positioning claim and belongs in `positioning.md`. The mission paragraph describes what is being done, not what is being promised.
